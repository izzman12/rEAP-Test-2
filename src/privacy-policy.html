Privacy & Security Policy

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

rEAP ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of personal and health-related information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect information on our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Provider Info: Name, NPI, contact details, payout info.
  • Client/Employee Info: Anonymous/pseudonymized identifiers, service usage.
  • Employer Info: Billing contacts, contract parameters.
  • Billing Records: Session data, invoices, payment status.
  • Usage Data: Login logs, IP address, audit trails.

3. Use of Information

We use collected data to manage billing, generate reports, and ensure compliance. We do not sell or use data for advertising.

4. Data Sharing

Data is shared only with authorized users (providers, employers), legal entities (if required), and HIPAA-compliant vendors or processors.

5. Data Retention

We retain PHI and billing data for a minimum of 7 years per HIPAA and state law requirements.

6. Your Rights

Clients and providers may request access, corrections, or disclosure logs of their information.

Contact us at: privacy@reapplatform.com

Security Policy

1. Overview

rEAP applies administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect health and billing data under HIPAA standards.

2. Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption (AES-256, TLS 1.2+)
  • Role-based access controls (RBAC)
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
  • Audit logging of all system access and data changes

3. Infrastructure Security

We host on HIPAA-compliant cloud infrastructure (AWS or Azure), maintain isolated environments, and perform regular security patching.

4. Incident Response

We respond to any suspected breach within 24 hours, notify affected parties as required, and perform full remediation and policy updates.

5. Employee & Vendor Compliance

  • All employees receive HIPAA training annually.
  • All vendors must sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs).
  • Access is revoked immediately upon role termination.

6. Contact

Questions or reports can be directed to: security@reapplatform.com
